The Tradition

A Dining Institution

Bones has long been recognized as the best steakhouse in Atlanta. Known for business lunches and celebratory dinners, we provide an experience that combines old-school charm with culinary excellence.

From our legendary Bone-In Ribeye to the freshest Maine Lobster flown in daily, every dish is prepared with precision. Our wine cellar, honored by Wine Spectator, houses an extensive collection of Old and New World vintages.
